Pinterest

Pinterest uses computer vision, an application of AI where computers are taught to “see”, in order to automatically identify objects in images (or “pins”) and then recommend visually similar pins.

Other applications of machine learning at Pinterest include spam prevention, search and discovery, ad performance and monetization, and email marketing.
