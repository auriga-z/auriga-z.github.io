Tips for south east asia travel

  • Purchase data SIM cards unless you have an international travel plan (ex. Telkomsel for Indonesia & Happy SIM for Thailand)
  • Pack light and include travel tissue, hand sanitizer, universal adapter, bug spray and necessary medicine (in case of food poisoning or headaches, etc)
  • Squat toilets are normal.
  • Bring cover ups, lightweight pants, and flip flops to visit temples (Ladies are forbidden to enter temple during menstruation)
  • Purchase thin raincoats there or bring your own & bring a hoodie for hikes
  • Always have cash in the foreign country’s currency
  • Rent a motorbike at your own risk - Always wear a helmet and remember to drive on the left side of the road
  • Traffic lights (if any) are often ignored in small towns
  • Exchange money at local ATMs for best exchange rate & have US cash to exchange for emergencies
  • Always bargain with local vendors, never pull out all of your cash at once, and give exact change
  • Check weather daily for random rain storms
  • Don’t expect refunds...for anything.
  • Agree on a price for transportation BEFORE going for the ride to avoid being overcharged. Remember to bargain!.
  • Don’t be alarmed if locals snap photos of you
  • Once you arrive at your home, drop a pin and save it to your phone
